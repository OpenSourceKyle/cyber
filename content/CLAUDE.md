# Cyber Notes
These are pentesting notes for HTB CPTS and OSCP prep.

## Structure
- `Docs/` = actual notes and references — this is the source of truth for techniques, methodology, and commands
- `Labs - TryHackMe/` = historical lab walkthroughs, read-only; records of exactly what was done during a room, not meant as notes or references
- `Labs - HackTheBox/` = historical lab walkthroughs, read-only; records of exactly what was done during a lab, not meant as notes or references

When looking for a technique, tool, or methodology — always search `Docs/` first. The Labs folders are for reviewing past work only.
